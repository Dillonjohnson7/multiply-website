Multiply builds intelligent manufacturing systems in America. We believe the factory floor is ready for a fundamental shift. Not another piece of fixed automation, but something that learns.

Every manufacturer we've spoken to faces the same constraint. The work is physical, repetitive, and increasingly difficult for staff. The people who know how to do it are retiring without a backfill pipeline. Machines designed to replace these workflows are expensive, inflexible, and take months to deploy.

Our approach is different. We work at the intersection of human skill and machine learning. What takes months with traditional automation takes hours with ours, and it adapts when the task changes.

We are building for the factories that legacy robotics forgot, the small and mid-size operations that make up the backbone of American manufacturing. Flexible, low-cost, and designed to multiply existing teams and equipment, not replace them.
